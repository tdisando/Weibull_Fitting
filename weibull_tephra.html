Plume Height Estimator

Enter your own lithic-size measurements (max clast diameter in cm vs √isopleth area in km). The tool fits a Weibull curve, derives λML, then estimates plume height via Ht = 5.01 · λML0.55 (Eq. 7, Bonadonna & Costa 2013).

Recommended: 1–20 cm
Range: 0.5 – 2.0
